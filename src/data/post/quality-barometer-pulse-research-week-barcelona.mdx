From December 8-11, 2025, we participated with the [Measurement Lab (M-Lab)](https://www.measurementlab.net/) team in the [Internet Society](https://www.internetsociety.org/) Pulse for Pulse Research Week.

Throughout Pulse Research Week, we met with diverse stakeholders and experts who are passionate about improving Internet connectivity worldwide.

Pavlos Sermpezis, founder of PiQ Mind and M-Lab’s Director, Technical Lead, presented the Internet Quality Barometer prototype at the [Giga/UNICEF](https://giga.global/) office in Barcelona.

Learn more about the Internet Quality Barometer framework at M-Lab’s [blog post](https://www.measurementlab.net/blog/iqb/#measurement-lab-publishes-the-internet-quality-barometer-framework) and the official [github repository](https://github.com/m-lab/iqb).
