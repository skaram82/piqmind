The Employment Trends Observatory, developed in collaboration with [exanta](https://exanta.io/) and the [Labour Geography Research Lab](https://lgrl.aegean.gr/), is an interactive dashboard designed to help users explore how employment evolves across the EU-27. From youth unemployment and part-time work to NEET and temporary employment, the tool brings together key indicators to provide a clear and data-driven picture of the labor market. With visualizations and searchable tables, it makes complex employment statistics accessible, comparable, and actionable.
